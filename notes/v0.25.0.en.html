What's new in v0.25.0

The biggest endgame update since v0.23.0: the heroic Nythraxis raid and its Deathless Court, a real signature ability for all 27 specializations, 192 achievements in the Book of Deeds and the Honor & Warfare PvP progression. Click a bubble for the detailed rules.

01

The heroic Nythraxis raid — read before the next raid night

The Deathless Court Raid

The Nythraxis raid (10 players) gets its heroic tier: after Deathless Rage, three spirits join the fight.

The three spirits' mechanics

Heroic raid loot Loot

Set pieces upgraded to ilvl 33, legendaries to ilvl 37 (procs preserved), and an exclusive weapon on every kill.

All the rewards

Fairer loot & lockout QoL

A disconnected winner no longer loses their item: it returns to the corpse. Eligible ghosts can reclaim their loot.

The new rules

Key takeaways for raid nights

  • Still 10 players — same comp, a difficulty above.
  • Malric's heal only stops on a real interrupt — good thing every class has one now (section 02).
  • Voss ignores taunts: crowd control is back on duty.

03

Book of Deeds — achievements have arrived

192 achievements System

Shift+Z opens the book: 2,365 Renown to earn across progression, combat, dungeons, delves, PvP, exploration… and hidden feats.

The full tour of the system

19 titles to show off

  • Titles render everywhere: nameplates, target frames, chat, friends and guild rosters, inspect cards, leaderboards.
  • 3 badge borders for the character sheet.
  • Public Renown board with self rank and percentile.

Retroactive, without the spam

  • Progress you already made is credited automatically — no wall of banners on first login.
  • New lifetime counters start from the release.
  • Three Chronicler NPCs in the world, including Saul the Chronicler.
04

PvP — Honor & Warfare

Persistent Honor Currency

Ranked Arena wins, Fiesta combat and match results award Honor that never expires.

How you earn it

FURY, quartermaster Vendor

40 epic ilvl 28 pieces: 4 complete sets, rings, necklaces and 3 weapons — paid in Honor.

The catalogue and prices

Warfare rating

  • Every PvP piece carries offensive and defensive Warfare: more damage dealt to players, less damage taken from players.
  • Separate internal caps for offense and defense.
  • No effect in PvE — the site's raid BiS is unchanged.
05

WOC Store & Season 1 Armory

The chest becomes the store

  • The chest opens on a combined Store + Daily Rewards experience (the Daily Rewards tab stays right beside it).
  • Claudium packs with Card, SOL and WOC purchase rails, shown based on availability.
  • Purely cosmetic: none of it grants in-game power.

28 weapon skins, account-wide

  • Eight weapon families: sword, axe, mace, dagger, staff, wand, bow, crossbow.
  • Four collections — Guildmark, Emberwrought, Hoarfrost, Fallen Star — from uncommon to legendary presentation.
  • Ownership is account-wide, with a loadout per weapon type.
06

Professions & gathering

Gathering (really) levels you up

  • Gathering and crafting now grant character XP, through the normal progression path.
  • Rewards scale with content level, with a green-to-gray falloff: trivial farming grants nothing.

Nodes in every zone

  • Every leveling zone now has ore, wood and herb nodes.
  • Mirefen gets a complete spread, Thornpeak its very first nodes.
  • In Eastbrook, ore points toward the Copper Dig.
07

The rest of the update

Social & safety

  • Personal mute: hides a player's public chat and overhead bubble, without breaking the friendship.
  • Names in chat are clickable (and tappable on mobile).
  • /play entry goes online-only, with first-class Discord login.

Sound & visuals

  • New SFX Studio (internal tool) and hot-deployable sound packs.
  • Rabbits, squirrels, songbirds, fish, gathering nodes, the mailbox and delve props move to optimized GLBs — lighter, smoother.
  • All 5 equippable bags plus the backpack get real painted 128px art.

Localization

  • 7,217 localized keys (151,557 locale rows in total).
  • All 21 non-English languages remain at zero pending entries.

Stability & tech

  • Lost-pet route recovery is bounded to 96 yards.
  • Automatic WebSocket reconnection when the mobile app returns to the foreground.
  • Production CPU incident capture with correlated evidence.
  • Full validation: 13,383 tests passed across 1,060 files.
