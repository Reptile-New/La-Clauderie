07

The rest of the update

Mobile 4 waves

  • Action ring rework (joystick + consumables).
  • Adaptive landscape/portrait layout.
  • Full window parity, compact minimap.

Online play & netcode

  • Movement prediction (instant key response, 350 ms cap).
  • No more collision strobing at high ping.
  • Need/greed loot rolls shown in real time.

World & rendering

  • Terrain LOD seams closed (gaps up to 3.3 yards).
  • Baked wolf models (mobs, druid, pets).
  • Groundwork for a voxel terrain engine (future tunnels).

Localisation & audit

  • Czech: 22nd language (6,045 strings), 0 pending across all 22.
  • Accuracy audit of all 157 ability tooltips.
  • Signed desktop launchers (Linux, macOS), Linux download re-enabled.
