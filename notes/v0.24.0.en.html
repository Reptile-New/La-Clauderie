The UI overhaul

19 unified windows Interface

Bags, bank, vendors, spellbook, talents… every window moves to the same frame: draggable, resizable, opaque.

What actually changes

Rebuilt Escape menu Settings

Categories, a global settings search, real modifier keybinds, and the game's first gamepad menu navigation.

Modernised keyboard Controls

Q/E become action buttons, Shift+1…0 reaches the 2nd bar. Your custom binds don't move.

The new default layout

Also on the UI side

  • Vendors with Buy / Sell / Buyback tabs.
  • Talents as classic tier-based picks.
  • Hostile mob nameplates cut off at 40 yards (less screen noise).
  • The field-of-view (FOV) slider finally works.
04

Group life & comfort

Ready checks Group

The leader types /ready: a chime, 30 seconds, and an anonymous group tally.

How to use it in raids

Little extras

  • /playtime: your character's total play time.
  • 5 rebindable keys for the pet bar (hunter/warlock).
  • Damage numbers finally all show on “Low” graphics.
  • The action bar also greys out targets that are too close (minimum range).
05

Performance & loading

Smooth crowds +35%

  • Each character goes from ~9 draw calls to just 1.
  • 90 players on screen: 45 → 61 fps.
  • You, your target and anything casting stay animated every frame.

Much faster loading

  • Only nearby terrain is built before you enter the world — the rest streams in the background.
  • Gameplay tips while loading.

Lighter server

  • Aura serialization costs half as much.
  • Fewer latency spikes at peak hours.
06

Mobile & native apps

Touch controls

  • Autorun locks by pushing the joystick all the way — no more separate button.
  • Two-finger zoom is more deliberate, and the camera no longer locks up after a pinch.
  • Portrait mode is removed: a “rotate your phone” screen.

Native sign-in

  • Discord sign-in and linking directly in the iOS/Android apps.
  • Native Sign in with Apple on iOS.
  • Password reset by email from the login screen.
07

The rest of the update

Professions

  • New introduction quest “A Trade for Every Hand” from Foreman Odell in Eastbrook (5 ores → 150 XP, 50 copper).
  • The crafting window finally groups recipes by profession.
  • Enchanting is built into the engine (disenchanting + 12 enchants) but not yet enabled in game.
  • Mail lets you choose the quantity of an attached stack.

The jail 🚔 Moderation

Moderators can send a player to jail — a real cell in the world, with inmate brawling.

The jail rulebook

Localisation & platforms

  • All 21 non-English languages reach 0 pending strings (2,596 translated for this version).
  • Fixes for real mistranslations in 16 languages.
  • Linux desktop builds (AppImage, .deb) are back.

What is NOT in v0.24.0

  • The Warrior rework (dual wield, parry, reworked rage) was merged then reverted within the same window — it will return reworked.
  • No new items, mobs, quests or zones: game data is identical to v0.23.0.
