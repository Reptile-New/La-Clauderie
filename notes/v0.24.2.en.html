What's new in v0.24.2

A reliability patch: no more heroic kills that lock you out without paying, loot rolls that keep their promises on mobile, and a one-time cleanup of frame positions left over from the reverted overhaul.

Released July 12, 2026 Heroic dungeons & mobile Reliability

01

Heroic rewards: no more lost races

A whole family of races between the final-boss kill, the save and party changes could leave a player locked out without a reward. Every path is closed.

Guaranteed Marks Heroic

A final-boss kill can no longer persist a lockout while losing the corresponding Heroic Mark, corpse loot or Need/Greed award.

The exact rules

Reliable rolls on mobile Interface

Roll prompts are reconciled server-side after a dropped submit, and short landscape phone screens now show only what's actionable.

Buttons, Bags and short screens

What does not change

  • Heroic drop tables are intentionally unchanged.
  • Each heroic final boss keeps its two guaranteed dedicated epic roll groups.
02

Unit frames: the one-time reset

A single-shot cleanup

  • Frame positions saved during the brief v0.24.0 overhaul replayed against the restored classic layout, leaving the player frame floating in a stale spot.
  • A one-time client-side reset clears them (player and target), gated by an epoch marker — it never re-clears, even on a downgrade.

What next?

  • Re-drag your frames from the corner move button, to taste.
  • Re-saved positions persist normally.
